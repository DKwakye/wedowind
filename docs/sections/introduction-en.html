Introduction back to ToC

As data-driven methods proliferate in wind energy research, benchmarking challenges have multiplied without standardized ways to compare results. WeDoWind provides a semantic framework that connects challenges, tasks, turbines, and submissions with evaluation metrics and leaderboard entries. By formalizing these relationships, it enables reproducible benchmarking, cross-challenge analysis, and improved discovery of computational methods for wind farm optimization and failure detection. The ontology supports interoperability and reuse of data across different platforms and competitions, facilitating better organization, sharing, and analysis of benchmarking results in the wind energy community.

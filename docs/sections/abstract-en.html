Abstract

WeDoWind is a lightweight ontology for modeling wind energy benchmarking challenges. It defines core entities—Challenges, Tasks, Submissions, Methods, and Evaluation Metrics—and their relationships to standardize how competitions, results, and computational approaches are described and compared in the wind energy domain. The ontology supports interoperability and reuse of data across different platforms and competitions, facilitating better organization, sharing, and analysis of benchmarking results in the wind energy community.
